Fisrt, let's discover the four lands of Disneyland Park !

Fantasyland

A magical world inspired by classic Disney stories. Amongst castles, carousels and colourful scenery, you will find gentle attractions such as It's a Small World, Peter Pan's Flight or Snow White. It's the perfect land for dreamers and their families.

Adventureland

An exotic setting inspired by jungles, pirates and explorers. Here you will find Pirates of the Caribbean, Indiana Jones and Robinson's Cabin. A mysterious atmosphere full of adventure.

Frontierland

Immersed in the atmosphere of the Wild West, this land recreates a pioneer town with saloons, gold mines and cowboys. It's home to Big Thunder Mountain and Phantom Manor, two of the park's most iconic attractions.

Discoveryland

A futuristic land combining science fiction and time travel. With Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain, Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast and Autopia, it's the perfect place for fans of technology and space.

Now that we have explored Disneyland Park, let us set off to discover Walt Disney Studio Park !

Walt Disney Studio Park

This second Disneyland Paris park takes visitors behind the scenes of cinema and animation. It offers immersive attractions such as Ratatouille: The Totally Crazy Adventure of Remy, Crush's Coaster and Avengers Assemble: Flight Force. The atmosphere is more modern, with an emphasis on Pixar, Marvel and Disney films. A major new addition is in the works: a land dedicated to Frozen is currently under construction, promising total immersion in the kingdom of Arendelle.

Essential Attractions In The Park

Disneyland Paris is packed with iconic attractions to suit all tastes. From thrilling adventures such as Pirates of the Caribbean, Big Thunder Mountain and Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain to gentler, more magical experiences such as It's a Small World and Ratatouille, every visitor is guaranteed an unforgettable experience.

It's a Small World

A boat ride across the continents, accompanied by a catchy song and colourful scenery representing cultures from around the world. A gentle attraction full of good cheer.

Pirates of the Caribbean

Embark on an adventure into the heart of a pirate world: cannons, treasures, ships and a mysterious atmosphere await you in this immersive, cult attraction.

Annual Special Events

Two seasons completely transform the atmosphere of the park each year. At Halloween, pumpkins, ghosts and Disney villains invade the paths, creating a mysterious and fun atmosphere with unique decorations and exclusive shows. In December, the magic of Christmas takes over: giant Christmas trees, illuminations, festive music and winter parades immerse visitors in an enchanted fairy tale.

Halloween

Every autumn, Disneyland Paris dons its most beautiful spooky decorations to celebrate Halloween. Pumpkins, spider webs and lanterns fill the paths, while Disney Villains take centre stage for unforgettable encounters. Exclusive shows and entertainment give the park a mysterious yet fun atmosphere, perfect for thrilling visitors... with a smile.

Christmas

From November onwards, the magic of Christmas transforms the park into a truly enchanted village. Fairy lights adorn every street, a huge Christmas tree stands proudly at the entrance, and festive music accompanies visitors throughout their day. Christmas parades, snow falling on Main Street, and Disney characters dressed in winter clothes make this one of the most magical times of the year.
